Alexandra “Alex” Wehrley (born July 29, 1988) is an American TV host, actress and former Miss Wisconsin USA. She is best known for co-hosting the 2015 Miss USA pageant.

Alex started modeling when she was 9 years old. She graduated from the University of Wisconsin–Madison in 2009, with a B.A. in communications.

Alex made her big screen debut in the movie Rudderless (2014). She played the role of a reporter in that movie. Alex produced some of her own contents including short-films like “Let it go”. She has hosted TV shows for multiple platforms. She hosted Tribune Media’s Eye Opener, as well as the talk show Oklahoma LIVE. After heading to Hollywood, she began working as a freelancer for E! News Now and has also co-hosted the CMA Awards red carpet and VH1’s Big Morning Buzz with Nick Lachey.

Personal

Wehrley is married to comedian, journalist, and creator of “Manitowoc Minute”, Charlie Berens.
