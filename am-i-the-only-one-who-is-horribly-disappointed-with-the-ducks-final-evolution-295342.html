Am I the only one who is horribly disappointed with the ducks final evolution? | Page 2

Publish date: 2024-07-22
I shall now repeat once more that it’s not that I don’t like Quaquaval’s design (okay I kinda do a bit), I’m just massively disappointed is all.

If anyone wants to know how I would like it, well, just make it look like an actual animal for once, make it more like an actual peacock instead of peacock feathers randomly floating around it, and possibly cut out some overdesigned elements too… just, many things in general that I’d be here all day explaining.

Hell, Imma go re-quote the user that said they now hate all duck Pokemon. Another reason I don’t like Quaquavel is because I think it also feels to similar to the other water fowls in Pokemon, especially if you take away from some parts of the design.

If they really wanted to do the carnival dancer theme, then maybe, just don’t do it with a duck starter. Howabout instead, a beaver water starter that uses wood it created as costume-like props? Not only would we have less mons to compare it with, but that actually sounds like a much better idea! But of course don’t make it overly humanoid.

Seriously this duck, if we cut out all the bad stuff and improved it, it possibly would have been amazing. I actually think a lot of the concept is really cool, but of course it is wasted on a lame design. And just thinking about what could have been just makes me irritated. I really don’t want to not like duckster, but I can’t help but think of ways it would have been so much better… as well as it’s name. Which I pronounced several different times at this point.
