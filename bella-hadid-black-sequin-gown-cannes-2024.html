Bella Hadid Wins Cannes in a Glamorous Black Sequin Gown

Publish date: 2024-09-21

Bella Hadid was an absolute vision as she debuted her most glamorous red-carpet look of the year so far to close out the 77th Cannes Film Festival.

After stepping out in two incredible sundresses for a sunny day on the beaches of the French Riviera, the supermodel hit the red carpet at the Palais des Festivals in an incredible black gown courtesy of Atelier Versace.

Anadolu//Getty Images

The archival halterneck dress was broken up into two tones of black, crafted from different materials. The diamond-shaped bodice was made from a sheer black layered silk tulle that cinched Hadid tightly at the waist. The floor-length skirt—which featured a vertical stripe pattern made of alternating strips of embroidered black sequins and black beads—flowed off her hips.

Stephane Cardinale - Corbis//Getty Images

The piece hails from the Atelier Versace Spring/Summer 2001 collection, and the model wore it to attend the premiere of romantic comedy L’Amour Ouf.

Stephane Cardinale - Corbis//Getty Images

Hadid paired the fantastic gown with a gauzy black scarf, which she rested on her arms, and layers of diamonds: white diamond chandelier earrings, a white diamond ring, and a cocktail ring featuring a large ruby surrounded by smaller white diamonds.

More from Bella

The Orebella founder had her hair up in a slicked-back, curly updo. She wore rosy makeup, wispy lashes, a glossy pink lip, and a nude manicure.

Stephane Cardinale - Corbis//Getty Images

Hadid has been showing off some of her best fashion ever at Cannes this month, including a gauzy naked dress and two gleaming golden gowns that made her glow like the sun. The annual film festival wraps up this Saturday, May 25.
