Brody Jenner has a fun-filled day at Disneyland with his mother Linda Thompson and brother Brandon

Publish date: 0001-01-01

The Hills star Brody Jenner was spotted enjoying a fun-filled day at Disneyland in Anaheim, California, on Wednesday.

The 37-year-old reality television personality was first pictured with his mother Linda Thompson while going on a teacup ride, and the mom-son duo was seen taking a selfie together.

The Princes Of Malibu cast member was later spotted covering up the 71-year-old’s eyes as they plunged downward on a log ride while his older brother Brandon tightly held on to one of his children.

Having fun: Brody Jenner was spotted spending quality time with his mother, Linda Thompson and his older brother Brandon at Disneyland on Wednesday afternoon

Jenner kept it casual during the trip to Disneyland, as he wore a white graphic-printed t-shirt for the trip.

At one point, the reality television figure donned a pair of rectangular black sunglasses and a Goofy-themed baseball cap.

His lengthy light brown hair draped downwards towards his shoulders as he spent time with his mother, who had previously donned a pink t-shirt.

The former Hee Haw series regular also sported a pair of stylish sunglasses while enjoying the teacup ride, and her beautiful blonde hair was at one point held back by a white visor.

Loved-up duo: At one point, the reality television personality and his mother were spotted taking a selfie together while enjoying a tea cup ride

Dressed for the occasion: The son of Caitlyn Jenner was seen wearing a Goofy-themed cap while spending time with his family

Brandon, 40, kept it similarly casual as he hung out with his mother and brother while wearing a long-sleeve white t-shirt, a pair of blue shorts and a baseball cap.

Jenner is well known for his time on the hit MTV reality series, The Hills, which ran for a total of six seasons that aired from 2006 until 2010.

The program was focused on the lives of several Los Angeles-based young adults and featured figures such as Lauren Conrad, Whitney Port and Heidi Montag, among several others.

The model was a supporting cast member for five out of the show’s six seasons and returned as part of the main set of subjects for the series’ continuation, entitled The Hills: New Beginnings.

Back at it: Jenner is a regular cast member on The Hills’ reboot, The Hills: New Beginnings, which premiered on MTV in 2019

Several of the original figures from The Hills returned for the reboot, and several  newcomers, including the reality television personality’s ex-girlfriend, Kaitlynn Carter, signed onto the new version of the program.

Thompson and her older son, as well as personalities such as Pamela Anderson and Kristin Cavallari, appear on The Hills: New Beginnings as guest stars.

The show’s first season premiered in June of 2019 and was met with mixed critical attention, with many viewers pointing to the relatively unchanged format of the series as one of its weak points. 

Familiar faces: Several cast members from the original series, including Heidi Montag and Audrina Partridge, returned for the show’s reboot

Mixed reception: The Hills: New Beginnings made its initial debut in 2019 and was met with middling critical reviews

During a recent sit-down with Entertainment Tonight, Jenner discussed filming with Cavallari, whom he previously dated, and remarked that, although the two have a history with each other, they had patched things up and were on friendly terms. 

‘Kristin and I, we have a past… I love Kristin, she’s great. You know, we get along, and she’s a lot of fun, for sure,’ he expressed.

The reality television figure then noted that, although Brandon was happy about being a father, he was content to take care of them and not to start a family himself. 

‘My brother has three kids, so I enjoy playing the uncle role…that’s not my main focus right now. Kids aren’t my main focus,’ he said.
