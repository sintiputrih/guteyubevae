Tragic Loss: Bryson Walker Killed in Rollover Crash at Dumas Hwy. and Central Ave.

A devastating single-vehicle rollover accident claimed the life of Bryson Walker, 34, in the early hours of Saturday, September 30th. The incident occurred at the intersection of Dumas Highway and Central Avenue on the northbound access road in Amarillo. Here are the details surrounding this heartbreaking event.

Details of the Accident:

On Saturday, September 30th, at 3:24 a.m., Amarillo police officers were dispatched to the scene of a reported single-vehicle rollover crash at the intersection of Dumas Highway and Central Avenue on the northbound access road. The Amarillo Police Department (APD) swiftly responded to the call.

Upon arrival, officers found that other first responders and Potter County officials were already at the accident scene, indicating the gravity of the situation. The driver involved in the tragic accident was identified as Bryson Walker, aged 34.

Walker had been traveling north on Dumas Highway and had taken the exit at Central Avenue. Tragically, he lost control of his vehicle, resulting in a rollover. During the course of the accident, Walker was ejected from his truck, leading to severe injuries.

Bryson Walker was immediately transported to a local hospital, where medical professionals fought diligently to save his life. Despite their efforts, Walker succumbed to his injuries and tragically passed away. His family has been notified, and their grief is immeasurable.
