Can home heating oil freeze?

Publish date: 2024-07-19

Home heating oil technically doesn’t ‘freeze’ in that it becomes a solid mass – however, when the temperature drops to -30oC, it can turn into a gel-like consistency (due to the presence of wax in the oil), which makes it less fluid and can lead to clogging in your pipes.Click to see full answer.
