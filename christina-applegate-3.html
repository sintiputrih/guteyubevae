Career

Christina Applegate made her debut in 1972 in a television serial called Days of our lives. She starred in it with her mother. She was even a part of television commercials for baby bottles from the brand Playtex. She made her feature film debut in 1981 with a movie called Jaws of Satan. In 1983 she was part of a movie called Grace Kelly.

In 1985 she was part of a television series called Washington. Subsequently she was also a part of other television series like Charles in Charge, Father Murphy, Silver spoons. From 1986-1986 she was part of a television series called Heart of the City. She has done special appearances in many television series including All is forgiven, Amazing stories, Family ties.

She rose to fame by starring in a television series called Married with children from 1987-1997. Simultaneously she starred in some movies like Dance till Dawn, Streets. She also had a small role in 21 Jump street and Top of the Heap. Apple gate hosted Saturday Night Live in 1993.

She gave another brilliant performance in 1991 in a movie called Don’t tell Mom the babysitter’s dead. In 1995 she was a part of three movies namely, Vibrations, Across the moon, Wild bill. After that she starred in Mars Attacks (1996) and Nowhere (1997). In 1998 she was part if a movie called Claudia’s return. In the same year she was part of a movie called The Big Hit and Jane Austen’s Mafia.

Apart from that, in the same year she started playing the lead character in a Television sitcom called Jesse. In 2001 starred in a comedy movie called Just Visiting. After that she starred in movies like Prince Charming (2001), The Sweetest Thing (2002), View from the top (2003), Wonderland (2003), Grand Theft Parsons (2003), Employee of the month (2004), Anchorman: The legend of Ron Burgandy (2004) and it’s subsequent spin off.

In 2002 and 2003 she was also a part of two episodes of Friends. She played the role of Rachel (Jennifer Aniston’s) sister. Apart from television and movies Christina has done significant theatre and stage plays as well. In 2004 she was a part of a Broadway musical called Sweet Charity. From 2007-2009 she was a part of a television sitcom called Samantha Who?.
