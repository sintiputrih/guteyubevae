Use Kohls Store coupons On The Kohls App

Here's how to use store coupons on the Kohl's app:

  • Install the Kohl’s app on your smartphone and sign in or create a new account.
  • Select the “Wallet” feature to store all your Kohl's cash, rewards, and coupons in one place. 
  • Add coupons to your wallet by adding your physical coupon code manually in the app or scanning the barcode of the digital Kohl’s coupon. If you're signed into your Kohl's account when you earn Kohl's Cash or rewards online, they should automatically appear in your app wallet.
  • Shop as you normally would within the app and add items to your cart.
  • When you're ready to check out, access your Wallet, select the coupons you wish to use, and watch the discount apply automatically to your total purchase.
