Depeche Mode Kicks Off North American Leg Of Acclaimed THE DELTA MACHINE WORLD TOUR Tomorrow, August

Publish date: 2024-06-24

LOS ANGELES, Aug. 21, 2013 /PRNewswire/ — Depeche Mode are set to launch their acclaimed THE DELTA MACHINE WORLD TOUR in North America tomorrow, August 22 at Detroit’s DTE Energy Music Theatre.  One of the world’s most influential and innovative bands continues to draw huge legions of loyal fans to arenas and stadiums to experience their unforgettable performances.  Produced by Live Nation, the North American leg of THE DELTA MACHINE WORLD TOUR will take Depeche Mode to 21 cities across the continent including sold-out shows in New York City, Los Angeles, Toronto and Las Vegas.  Tickets for several shows are still on sale now at www.livenation.com.

Depeche Mode wrapped up their massively successful European summer stadium tour in July that played to 1.3 million fans at 38 sold-out shows across 25 countries.  The tour recently earned the band the top spot on Billboard’s “Hot Tours Chart.”  With tickets to see the critically acclaimed show still in high demand, the band announced they will return to Europe this winter for an arena tour with shows lined up in London, Dublin, Belfast, Paris, Amsterdam, Oslo, Milan, Madrid and even more dates set for the Middle East and Eastern Europe as well. 

For an all access, behind-the-scenes look at THE DELTA MACHINE WORLD TOUR, fans can watch the band’s “VEVO Tour Exposed” piece HERE.   Bat For Lashes and Crystal Castles will act as support for the upcoming U.S. and Canadian shows.  A full list of dates on THE DELTA MACHINE WORLD TOUR is below.

Delta Machine
, Depeche Mode’s 13th studio album, is out now on Columbia Records. The album, recorded in Santa Barbara, CA and New York City, was produced by
Ben Hillier
and mixed by Flood. With their new release, Depeche Mode continues to be one of modern music’s most influential groups. Formed in 1981, Depeche Mode –
Martin Gore, Dave Gahan and Andy ‘Fletch’ Fletcher – have won critical and commercial acclaim around the world both in the studio and on the road.  The band’s 12 albums have reached the Top Ten in over 20 countries, including the U.S. and United Kingdom, and have global sales in excess of 100 million.
