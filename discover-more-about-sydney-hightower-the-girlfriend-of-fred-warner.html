Warner was named to the Pro Bowl and First-Team All-Pro as a rookie.

Warner continued to be a key contributor to the 49ers’ defense in 2019. He finished the season with 118 total tackles, 13 tackles for loss, 2.5 sacks, one interception, and one forced fumble. Warner was again named to the Pro Bowl.
