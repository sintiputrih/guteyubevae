Is Dua Lipa Dating Jack Harlow Now?

Publish date: 2024-05-30

Dua Lipa has been in her traveling, single-girl era since splitting from Anwar Hadid last year, but now, sources say she may be dating rapper Jack Harlow.

Harlow, who made his crush on Lipa clear by releasing a song titled "Dua Lipa" on his latest album, recently met and took a photo with the pop singer at Variety's 2022 Hitmakers Brunch. ("Dua Lipa, I'm tryna do more with her than do a feature," he raps in his track.)

Harlow said he called Lipa on FaceTime to get her blessing, and she awkwardly agreed, but since their meeting, the two have kept in "constant communication," per a Page Six source.

The insider added that Harlow was already "very interested in her, and was going to strongly pursue [the romance]." They added, "He is going to do his best, as he has always been a fan of her."

The rapper reportedly made a major move by flying to New York City to meet up with Lipa after her Z100 Jingle Ball appearance last Friday. The two even had lunch together in the Meatpacking District the next day, per the insider.

Lipa was previously rumored to be dating The Daily Show host Trevor Noah after the two were spotted on what looked like a low-key date in New York City, but the "Potion" singer never confirmed nor denied the rumors.

Harlow, meanwhile, was rumored to be dating TikTok star Addison Rae after they were spotted at an event together, but she denied the rumors on Twitter shortly after.
