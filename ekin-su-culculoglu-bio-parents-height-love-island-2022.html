Ekin-Su Cülcüloğlu believes in forever after. So, in search of that “real genuine spark,” this beauty joined Love Island for its eighth installment. She found the concept of the show to bring back “true and raw feelings.”

Get to know her better as this Ekin-Su Cülcüloğlu Bio proceeds. 

Ekin-Su Cülcüloğlu On Love Island 2022

ITV2’s Love Island 2022 premiered on June 6, 2022, and as always it saw a bunch of attractive males and females looking for love on the show.

But unlike the previous season, the pairing on season 8 was a little different. As the girls arrived at the villa, they were asked to line up in front of the pool and paired with the boy that the audience chose for them. Yes! This was the first time ever the public played cupid.

However, our star, Ekin-Su wasn’t one of those early birds who got a partner.

Joining Ekin-Su in the luxury Majorcan villa for Love Island 2022 were her co-contestants — Paige Thorne, Afia Tonkmor, Amber Beckford, Luca Bish, Andrew La Page, Gemma Owen, Davide Sanclimenti, Tasha Ghouri, Dami Hope, Indiyah Polack, Liam Llewellyn, and Ikenna Ekwonna.

Also, Missy Keating, Kat Harrison, Brad McDermott, Sophie Draper, Jordan Spencer, and Joshua Legrove were rumored to join the show in the latter half.

If case you didn’t know, Ekin-Su was also one of these rumored casts who was believed to be “the perfect contestant.” Even before ITV2 revealed the list, The Sun had talked about Ekin-Su, “Not only is she beautiful, she’s also used to being on TV and moves in showbiz circles so she’s sure to bring fireworks. She’ll have no shortage of guys after her in the villa, that’s for sure.”

All excited to appear on Love Island 2022, Ekin-Su took it to her IG on June 8 to write, “AHHH! The secret is out. Catch me on Love Island searching for the one! @loveisland @itv2.”

Who Is Ekin-Su Cülcüloğlu Dating Today?

Ekin-Su Cülcüloğlu didn’t reveal who she is dating today (as of June 2022). Well, given that Love Island had just begun, Ekin-Su must have been avoiding spoilers by keeping her love life out of the spotlight then.

However, Ekin-Su  was open about what she was looking for in a partner —  “I’m not just looking for looks, I’m looking for brains!” And this was also the reason why this beauty was single for so long before joining Love Island, “because I’m quite picky,” she admitted.

Ekin-Su wanted someone with intelligence, someone who she can have good chats with. But he shouldn’t have a shred of cockiness. She wanted “a serious man and a serious relationship.”

Determined to find her prince charming, Ekin-Su also confessed that she wasn’t afraid to tread on toes. Though Ekin-Su admitted to being on Love Island to make friends too, realistically, she wasn’t there to make seasonal girlfriends.

“I’m there to find the love of my life. I need to find the man of my dreams so if there is another girl in the way, I don’t care. I’m quite fiery and passionate so if I want something, I don’t care what gets in the way,” she explained.

But with this being said, there were rumors floating around that the English football player Jack Grealish had his eyes on Ekin-Su. Sadly, we have no information about them.
