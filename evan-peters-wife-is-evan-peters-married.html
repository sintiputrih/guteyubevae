Evan Peters Wife: Is Evan Peters Married?

Publish date: 2024-01-20

Evan Peters Wife: Is Evan Peters Married?: Evan Peters, officially known as Evan Thomas Peters is an American actor born on January 20, 1987, in St. Louis, Missouri to Phil Peters and Julie Peters.

He started his career at a younger age making his acting debut in the 2004 drama film Clipping Adam and starring in the ABC science fiction series Invasion from 2005 to 2006.

Evan Peters is best known for his multiple roles in Ryan Murphy’s FX anthology series American Horror Story, from 2011 to 2021. He played a supporting role in the superhero film Kick-Ass, and Peter Maximoff / Quicksilver in the X-Men film series.

His role as a detective in the HBO crime miniseries Mare of Easttown won him a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor. Evan Peters also received a nomination for the BIFA Award for Best Supporting Actor for playing a rebellious student in the heist film American Animals (2018).

In January 2023, Evan Peters made major headlines after winning the Best Actor in a Limited Series award at the 80th Golden Globes Awards which was held on Wednesday, January 11, 2023.

The nomination was his very first Golden Globes nod, which eventually turned into his very first Golden Globe win. This comes after Evan Peters dominated Netflix with his chilling portrayal of Jeffrey Dahmer in Dahmer – Monster.

He won the award over fellow nominees Taron Egerton (Black Bird), Colin Firth (The Staircase), Andrew Garfield (Under the Banner of Heaven), and Sebastian Stan (Pam & Tommy).

Evan Peters Wife: Is Evan Peters Married?

We are not very sure whether Evan Peters is married. However, in 2012, Peters began dating actress Emma Roberts, whom he met on the set of the film Adult World.

In March 2014, Peters confirmed that he and Emma Roberts were engaged. However, in March 2019, it was announced that Peters and Roberts had ended the relationship.

At the time of this writing, there was no information as to whether he was married or in a relationship.
