How do I connect SimpliSafe to New WiFi?

Publish date: 2024-04-30

To connect your All New SimpliSafe to WiFi: Step 1: Press the “menu” button on your Keypad and enter your 4 digit Master PIN when prompted. Step 2: Navigate to System Settings and select it by pressing the right rocker. Step 3: Navigate to WiFi and select it. Click to see full answer. Then, how do I change my WiFi on SimpliSafe?Just go into System Settings and select the “WiFi” entry. Choose your Wi-Fi network and press the “Use” command on the right side of the screen. Type in the password using the keypad and then hit the “Go” command. Give it a moment to connect, and after a few seconds you’ll be all set to go. can you set up SimpliSafe without WiFi? @cpisales1 Yes, SimpliSafe Yes, SimpliSafe has its own separate wireless communication between components. So WiFi is not necessary to trigger an alarm siren. If you do want to have Monitoring, though, the Base Station also has built-in cellular, which it can use to send signals to dispatch even without WiFi. Also to know is, should I connect SimpliSafe to WiFi? You do not need a computer or WIFI for SimpliSafe to work for your basic home security needs. However, WIFI and access to the SimpliSafe app through either Google Play Store or the App Store for iOS users is required for the SimpliCam or Video Doorbell Pro.How do I reconnect my SimpliSafe camera to WiFi?Click the gear icon to bring up your currently installed cameras. Select the camera you would like to update and it should slide to that particular camera’s settings screen. Finally, click “WiFi Network” and it should ask you for the name of the new WiFi network you would like to choose.
