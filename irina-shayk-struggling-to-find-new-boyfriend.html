Irina Shayk Is Struggling To Find New Boyfriend Following Tom Brady Split

As soon as Irina Shayk made her international modeling debut on the 2011 Sports Illustrated Swimsuit magazine cover, the Russian beauty became an instant star in the fashion world.

Fellow model Chrissy Teigen even hailed Shayk as the most beautiful woman in the world in a 2016 interview with Glamour Magazine. With a net worth of $25 million, as reported by Celebrity Net Worth, Shayk's success is undeniable. Given her beauty, talent, and wealth, fans find it surprising that she's reportedly struggling to find a boyfriend.

A source close to Irina Shayk made the shocking claim, telling PageSix about the model's current love life,

She is shopping, but her dating pool is a tiny group of people. She needs famous, good-looking, and money. How many of those are there? Five, ten?

The source added that the mother of one was reportedly looking to date someone from Europe or North America. PageSix also reported that actor Tom Cruise was one of the possible suitors Irina Shayk was considering. The news comes as reports claim Tom Cruise has split with Russian socialite Elsina Khayrova, whom he was only rumored to have been dating for two months.

As for Shayk, she broke up with retired football player Tom Brady in October 2023. According to a report by People Magazine, a source close to the former couple revealed the reason for their breakup,

They both keep having obligations, and it was getting more difficult to be in the same city at the same time.
