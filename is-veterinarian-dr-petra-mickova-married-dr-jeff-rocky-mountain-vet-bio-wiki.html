Dr. Petra Mickova Husband, Dr. Jeff Young

Jeff Young was born on the 14th April 1956, in Indiana USA, the son of Ester Stevens, who divorced his biological father and remarried to man named Jay. Jeff started his own clinic soon after graduation, and since then has improved and expanded his business. He is not only healing animals, but also rescuing them too, and has opened clinics in Mexico and the Czech Republic, as well as nearby Cortez, Colorado.
