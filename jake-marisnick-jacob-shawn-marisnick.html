Career

‘The Marlins’ boosted Jake and Christian Yelich to the major leagues from the Double-A Jacksonville Suns on July 23, 2013. He recorded his 1st major league hit on July 26, 2013, off Pittsburgh Pirates pitcher Jeff Locke. On July 31, 2013, he hit his 1st career home run in a game against the New York Mets.
