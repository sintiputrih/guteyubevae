Johnny Ruffo Cause Of Death, Age, Funeral

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Johnny Ruffo Cause Of Death, Age, Funeral: Australian singer, songwriter, dancer, actor, and television presenter, Johnny Ruffo, also known as John Patrick Ruffo has reportedly passed on.

Johnny Ruffo Cause Of Death

Johnny Ruffo died at age 35 on Friday, November 10, 2023, following a long battle with brain cancer. The news of his demise was announced in a statement via his official Instagram page.

“It is with a heavy heart that today we had to farewell our beloved Johnny. Surrounded by his partner Tahnee and family, Johnny went peacefully with the support of some incredible Nurses & Doctors”

“He was a very talented, charming and sometimes cheeky boy. Johnny was very determined and had a strong will. He battled all the way to the end and fought as hard as he could. Such a beautiful soul with so much more to give”

“We all love you Johnny and will remember you for all the joy you brought to our lives. Rest easy”

Ruffo rose to fame by participating in his country’s edition of The X Factor in 2011. He was the last contestant eliminated in the competition and subsequently signed a recording contract with Sony Music Australia.

The following year (2012), he won the country’s edition of Dancing With the Stars. Ruffo became a household name following his role in the soap; “Home and Away” which aired from 2013 to 2016.

After shooting to stardom for his role as Chris Harrington on “Home and Away”, he appeared in the TV miniseries; “House of Bond” in 2017.

Ruffo put his career on hold after his diagnosis in 2017. After two years of treatment, he entered remission, but his cancer returned in 2020. He wrote on Instagram in November 2020.

“After an unexpected week of seizures and excruciating headaches it is with a heavy heart that i have to let you know i now have another huge battle ahead of me as my brain cancer has returned, though i will dig deep and beat this shit disease again”

Johnny Ruffo Age

Johnny Ruffo celebrated his 35th birthday in March this year (2023) before he passed away in November. The Australian singer was born on March 8, 1988, in Balcatta, Australia.

Johnny Ruffo Funeral

At the time of this report, the family was planning his final funeral arrangement and the details are yet to be finalized. We will keep you updated.
