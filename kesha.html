Kesha aka Kesha Rose Sebert is an American singer, songwriter, rapper, and actress. As of 2022, Kesha’s net worth is $10 million. She accumulated her net worth through her singing, songwriting, and acting career. She is mainly known for her popular singles and albums like Righ Round, Animal, and Rainbow.

Along with singing popular songs, she has co-written some popular songs for renowned artists like Britney Spears, Ariana Grande, Miley Cyrus, Miranda Cosgrove, and others. Many of her songs and albums top the US Billboard charts and broke many records.

Kesha Wiki/Biography

Born on 1 March 1987, Kesha’s age is 35 years as of 2022. She was brought up and raised in a middle-class family from Los Angeles, California, United States. She is known to be an American by nationality and belongs to the Christianity religion. She completed her schooling at Franklin High School and Brentwood High School.

After that, she was enrolled at Barnard College of the Columbia University but she left after three months to focus on her music career. Since childhood, she was interested in music and singing and used to play trumpet and saxophone in her school’s marching band. She also took songwriting training from her mother when she was in high school.

Career

Kesha started her career as a singer and songwriter along with her mother and elder brother. She joined a band with her elder brother and co-wrote a song titled Stephen along with her mother when she was just 16 years old.

After that in the year 2005, she signed a deal with Kemosabe Records and Prescription songs. She sang the background vocals for the popular song titled Nothing in This World by Paris Hilton. Later, she signed a deal with DAS Communications Inc. management company and gained major label record deals while working with the company. She also co-wrote the song titled This Love by the popular Australian pop group named The Veronica.

In the year 2008, she made her appearance in the music video titled I Kissed a Girl with her close friend Katy Perry. She has also sung the background vocals of the song titled Lace and Leather by Britney Spears.

In the year 2009, she has signed a deal with RCA Records, and she worked for almost six years on her debut album and wrote about 200 songs for this album. Later in the year 2009, she released the lead single titled Tik Tok which broke so many records and became the highest sales week of a single in the United States. Other popular singles from her debut album are Blah Blah Blah, Your Love is My Drug, and Take if Off. In the same year, she has also been featured in two popular songs by musical artists like Taio Cruz and 3OH!3.

In the year 2010, she performed in a charity concert that was focused to raise funds for the victims of the 2010 Tennessee Floods and also performed as a supporting artist in the world concert named the Last Girl on Earth by a popular pop singer Rihanna. Later in the same year, she released an extended play titled Cannibal along with the lead single titled We R Who We R. After that another single from the extended play was released titled Blow and she headed towards her first world tour named Get Sleazy Tour.

In the same year, she co-wrote the popular song Till the World Ends for popular singer Britney Spears and has also been featured in the remix version of the song along with well-known rapper Nicki Minaj. She also wrote the lyrics of the popular song What Baby Wants and the album Welcome 2 My Nightmare for singer Alice Cooper.

In the year 2012, she released her second album titled Warrior along with singer Wayne Coyne. They also sang songs for the album titled The Flaming Lips and Heady Fwends for the band named The Flaming Lips together. In the same year, she has also released her autobiography named My Crazy Beautiful Life and released the lead single titled Die Young from her second album.

After that, she released the second single titled C’Mon from the album which didn’t perform commercially well. In the year 2013, she started her second world tour named Warrior Tour to promote her second album. The third single titled Crazy Kids from the album again became a huge hit and later in the same year a TV series titled Kesha My Crazy Beautiful Life started on the MTV channel. In the same year, she released another album titled Lipsha along with the band named The Flaming Lips. She has also released a single titled Timber along with the popular American rockstar Pitbull in the same year.

In the year 2014, she has made her appearance in the television series titled Jane the Virgin in a guest role, and the very next year she made a classic rock band named Yeast Infection and performed in various live stage performances. In the year 2016, she went on her third world tour named Kesha and the Creepies Fuck the World Tour and in the meantime, she has also recorded 22 songs for her third album.
