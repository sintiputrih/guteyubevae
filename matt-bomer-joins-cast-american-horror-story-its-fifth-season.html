Matt Bomer Joins the Cast of American Horror Story

Publish date: 2024-06-07

Matt Bomer is set to check in to the American Horror Story: Hotel. The actor confirmed that he’ll be joining the cast, which will also include Lady Gaga, during a PaleyFest panel last night in Los Angeles.

This isn’t the first time Bomer has appeared on the Ryan Murphy–run show. He made a guest appearance on Season 4. “I love collaborating with Ryan Murphy,” he said during the panel. “I had the time of my life and I told Ryan that. He said, 'Great, do you want to come work next season?' And I said, 'Hell, yes.'" The rest is television history. While he’s playing coy on what exactly his role will be, there are some rumors of romance between his and Lady Gaga’s characters. Only time will tell!

The fifth season will premiere on FX in October, and sadly it will be without one of our favorite actresses, Jessica Lange. She confirmed that she is leaving the series for good. "We've had a great run here," Lange said at PaleyFest. "Forty years of acting and it's never going to get better than this. Yes, I'm done. I loved this season. It was my absolute favorite."
