Biography:

Mikey Madison was born on March 25, 1999 in Los Angeles, USA. Prior to her acting career, she competed in professional horseback riding. In 2013, she made her acting debut in a short film. She landed a few more roles in other short films afterward. She then landed the role of Max Fox in the FX comedy-drama show Better Things, which she has been playing since 2016.

She next played Young Maddie in the television series Imposters. At the same time, she played the title role in the independent film Liza, Liza, Skies Are Grey. Madison went on to have performances in such movies as All Rise and Nostalgia. In 2019, she portrayed Susan “Sadie” Atkins in the movie Once Upon a Time in Hollywood. She also had a voice role in the movie The Addams Family.
