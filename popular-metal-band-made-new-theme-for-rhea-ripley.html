Popular Metal Band Made New Theme For Rhea Ripley

Publish date: 2024-09-15

One of the mainstays of Monday Night Raw, The Judgement Day, came on the scene in 2022 and made Finn Balor, Damian Priest, Rhea Ripley, and Dominik Mysterio players for WWE. Even though Alter Bridge’s “The Other Side” is used for tag matches, Balor, Priest, and Mysterio got upgrades in their theme music. Ripley is about to get the same treatment.

Click here for our complete coverage of WWE RAW this week.

Ripley’s new entrance song, still featuring the ‘This is my brutality’ sting, now uses male vocals instead of those of Ash Costello from the previous version. Chris Motionless, lead singer of the metal band Motionless In White, went to Twitter to confirm that he did perform the vocals for Ripley’s new theme, which is set to release on September 19 on various streaming platforms,

Huge announcement! I am so beyond thrilled to reveal that I had the honor of working with @WWE recording the vocals for @RheaRipley_WWE’s new entrance theme “Demon In Your Dreams.” It will be available worldwide on @Spotify, @AppleMusic and @YouTube next Monday, September 19.

Ripley then replied to the tweet, thanking him for performing the song. She even teased a possible live performance in the future. Maybe for a Wrestlemania singles match?

The wait is nearly over!

I still can’t believe that this was made possible. Thank you so much @ChrisMotionless for being a complete legend and badass! So incredibly grateful!

Who’s ready for a possible live performance?

Ripley’s theme hasn’t played in full yet on WWE television, but hearing the entire track should be fun since. Even with the lead Motionless in White at the helm, should be a great track for the gym.
