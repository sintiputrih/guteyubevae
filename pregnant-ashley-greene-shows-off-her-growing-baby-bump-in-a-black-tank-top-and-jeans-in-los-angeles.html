Pregnant Ashley Greene shows off her growing baby bump in a black tank top and jeans in Los Angeles

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Ashley Greene put her burgeoning baby bump on display on Tuesday, as she made her way to an ADR studio in Los Angeles to work on her new movie, Some Other Woman.

The Twilight star, 35, kept her maternity style simple for the day and showed off her growing bump in a form-fitting black tank top and a pair of light blue jeans.

The soon-to-be mom finalized her outfit with comfortable black flip flops and wore a large Dior tote on her shoulder.
