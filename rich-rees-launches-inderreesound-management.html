Rich Rees Launches InderReesound Management

Publish date: 2024-05-03

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Rich Rees and his business partner Paul Inder have launched a new artist management / production company, InderReesound Management.

The new firm will focus on developing new and rising talent in the contemporary music scene and they have already signed Los Angeles-based indie rockers, The Janks.

"I am very proud to say we have signed our first act, an up and coming band from Los Angeles called The Janks. They are one of the best young bands I have ever seen live, and their songs are incredibly unique. We are really excited about this band and their potential." Rees told CelebrityAccess.

"Paul is producing all of the tracks for the EP, which is scheduled for a June 1st release date, and I will be handling the day to day tasks. They are also heading out on their first national tour next week, and will play all over the country through out the summer. We plan to find them a home with the right booking agency in the next few months. They are quickly getting a national buzz, and we are starting to get several inquiries from the press. They will be featured in a People Magazine article slated for June 1st." Rees added.

Rees is an industry veteran and professional musician, who formerly ran an unofficial left coast office for Fantasma working with Steve Peck. Rees also has experience as a booker and an A&R rep and most recently, he spent a tenure at M.P.I. Inder is an experienced producer and the son of Motorhead's Lemmy Kilmister.

The offices for InderReesound will be located at:
