Snooki Confronts Khloe Kardashian About Dissing Her: Watch

Publish date: 2024-06-23

Can’t all the reality stars just get along? Nicole “Snooki” Polizzi is a guest on Kocktails With Khloé‘s Wednesday, February 3, episode, where she reveals that host Khloé Kardashian insulted her when they first met.

On the FYI series’ new episode, Khloé, 31, asks Polizzi, 28, to tell the story, so the Jersey Shore alum explains that years ago, Khloé’s older sister Kim Kardashian texted Polizzi and invited her over to the family’s home.

“[Kim] tapped you on the shoulder — she goes, ‘Khloé, look who’s here,'” Polizzi says. “And you looked at me up and down — I swear to God, I’m not lying — and you go, ‘What the f–k is she doing here? Are you kidding me, Kim?'”

The Nicole & Jionni’s Shore Flip star “didn’t know what to say,” so she just left.

“I was like, ‘Good night,'” she continues. “I was so upset.”

Khloé, for her part, claims to not remember the incident.

“First of all, I don’t know why KhloMoney came out at that moment,” the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star says. “KhloMoney’s my alter ego.”
