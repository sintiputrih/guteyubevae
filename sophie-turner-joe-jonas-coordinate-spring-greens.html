See Sophie Turner and Joe Jonas Color-Coordinate in Spring Greens

Publish date: 2024-05-31

It seems that the worst of winter has finally passed—and what better way to celebrate the arrival of spring than to wear green?

Sophie Turner and Joe Jonas got the memo. On Wednesday afternoon, the couple was spotted walking around the Los Angeles neighborhood of Los Feliz color-coordinating in different shades of green.

The Game of Thrones alum showed off her casually cool minimalist style in a lime-colored ribbed knit dress from Australian label Finders Keepers. The piece featured a collar, buttons going down the middle, and (the holy grail of all dresses) pockets. She accessorized with a pair of black mules from Vagabond and black shades from French eyewear designer Thierry Lasry.

The Jonas Brothers alum sartorially held his own next to his stylish wife, wearing a Golf Wang cardigan that depicted a pixelated landscape in different hues of grassy green, baby pink, and sky blue. He paired the statement piece with a white undershirt and coffee-colored pants, and accessorized with a silver chain-link necklace, a watch with a black leather strap, blue-tinted Ray-Ban sunglasses, and white Reebok sneakers.

MEGA

The couple, who married in May 2019 and have since welcomed the arrival of their baby daughter, Willa, are known for making a fashion statement during their joint appearances.

In an interview last spring with Vogue, Turner opened up about how they developed their styles. “He surprisingly puts in a little bit more effort than me,” she told the outlet. “He’s always embraced high fashion and dressing up. I thought it was psychotic how he would wear jeans during the pandemic, while we were just sitting at home. I was like, What kind of serial killer does that?”

She added that the two will occasionally swap clothes. “The hardest thing in my life is organizing the washing, because I have no idea whose is whose,” she explained. “We buy and wear the exact same things. We’re always swapping clothes, especially vintage tees.”
