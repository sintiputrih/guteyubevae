T.J. Miller net worth and salary: T.J. Miller is an American actor and stand-up comedian who has a net worth of $9 million dollars. T.J. Miller was born in Denver, Colorado on June 4, 1981. He went on to attend George Washington University. While at George Washington, he began performing with the campus comedy troupe, GWU Recess. In 2006, he began appearing as part of a podcast called, “Red Bar Radio”. In 2007, he began his professional on-camera career, with a role on the short-lived series, “Carpoolers”. Since then, he has gone back and forth between film and television projects, including “Curb Your Enthusiasm”, “Cloverfield”, “Extract”, “The Goods: Live Hard, Sell Hard”, “She’s Out of My League”, “How to Train Your Dragon” and its sequel, “Get Him to the Greek”, “Unstoppable”, “Gulliver’s Travels”, “Happy Endings”, “The League”, “The Gorburger Show”, “Seeking a Friend for the End of the World”, “Rock of Ages”, “The Goodwin Games”, and “High School USA!”. He also regularly appeared as a guest commentator on “Chelsea Lately”. Starting in 2014, T.J. Miller began appearing on the HBO series “Silicon Valley” as Erlich Bachman, a role that earned him widespread critical acclaim. He subsequently appeared in the films “Deadpool”, “The Emoji Movie”, “Office Christmas Party” and “Ready Player One”.
