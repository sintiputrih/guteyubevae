The Top 10 Kansas City Chiefs Players of all Time

Publish date: 0001-01-01

The Kansas City Chiefs, one of the most beloved and successful franchises in the National Football League (NFL), have seen a number of remarkable players don the red and gold over the years. From Hall of Famers to franchise icons, these individuals have left an indelible mark on the team's history. In this article, we will take a closer look at the top 10 Kansas City Chiefs players of all time.

1. Derrick Thomas

Derrick Thomas is widely regarded as one of the greatest outside linebackers in NFL history. He spent his entire career with the Chiefs from 1989 until his tragic death in 2000. Thomas was a true force on the field, known for his incredible speed and ability to pressure the quarterback. He was an integral part of the Chiefs' dominant defense during the 1990s and was inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame in 2009.

2. Len Dawson

Len Dawson is a legendary figure in Chiefs history. As the team's starting quarterback from 1962 to 1975, he led the Chiefs to their first Super Bowl victory in 1970. Dawson was known for his poise, accuracy, and ability to come through in clutch moments. He was named the game's Most Valuable Player in Super Bowl IV and was inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame in 1987.

3. Tony Gonzalez

Tony Gonzalez is widely regarded as one of the greatest tight ends in NFL history. He played for the Chiefs from 1997 to 2008 and was a dominant force throughout his career. Gonzalez holds numerous NFL records for tight ends, including the most career receptions, receiving yards, and touchdown catches. He was a 14-time Pro Bowler and was inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame in 2019.

4. Willie Lanier

Willie Lanier is considered one of the greatest middle linebackers in NFL history. He played for the Chiefs from 1967 to 1977 and was a key member of the team's dominant defense. Lanier was a hard-hitting and instinctive player who was known for his ability to read offenses and make game-changing plays. He was named to the Pro Bowl eight times and was inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame in 1986.

5. Jamaal Charles

Jamaal Charles is one of the most electrifying running backs in Chiefs history. He played for the team from 2008 to 2016 and was a dynamic playmaker. Charles possessed incredible speed and agility, making him a nightmare for opposing defenses. He holds the Chiefs' franchise records for career rushing yards and rushing touchdowns. Charles was a four-time Pro Bowler and is widely regarded as one of the greatest running backs of his era.

6. Bobby Bell

Bobby Bell is a true icon in Chiefs history. He played for the team from 1963 to 1974 and was a versatile and dominant player on defense. Bell played both linebacker and defensive end during his career and was known for his athleticism and ability to make big plays. He was a six-time Pro Bowler and was inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame in 1983.

7. Will Shields

Will Shields is one of the greatest offensive linemen in Chiefs history. He played for the team from 1993 to 2006 and was a model of consistency and durability. Shields started in every game he played during his career, a remarkable achievement. He was a 12-time Pro Bowler and was inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame in 2015.

8. Priest Holmes

Priest Holmes is one of the most prolific running backs in Chiefs history. He played for the team from 2001 to 2007 and was a key component of the high-powered Chiefs offense. Holmes set numerous records during his career, including the most rushing touchdowns in a single season. He was a three-time Pro Bowler and was named the NFL Offensive Player of the Year in 2002.

9. Otis Taylor

Otis Taylor is a legendary wide receiver who played for the Chiefs from 1965 to 1975. He was known for his size, speed, and ability to make acrobatic catches. Taylor was a key part of the Chiefs' offense during their Super Bowl-winning season in 1970. He was a three-time Pro Bowler and was inducted into the Chiefs Hall of Fame in 1987.

10. Emmitt Thomas

Emmitt Thomas is one of the greatest cornerbacks in Chiefs history. He played for the team from 1966 to 1978 and was a shutdown defender. Thomas was known for his exceptional speed, agility, and ability to read offenses. He was a five-time Pro Bowler and was inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame in 2008.

The Kansas City Chiefs have been blessed with numerous talented and influential players throughout their history. From Derrick Thomas and Len Dawson to Tony Gonzalez and Jamaal Charles, these individuals have left an indelible mark on the team's legacy. Their contributions on the field have not only brought success to the franchise but have also endeared them to Chiefs fans for generations to come.
