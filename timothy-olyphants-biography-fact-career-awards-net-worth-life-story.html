Timothy has earned his wealth from his roles in several films and TV programs. He started his professional career in “Playwrights Horizons” where he played the role of Tim Hapgood. He received the theatre world award for his performance. He then performed in several other plays like “Plunge” and “The SantaLand Diaries”. He got his first television role in the 77 Sunset Strip TV series. He was then selected to become a part of Mr. & Mrs. Smith, a spy series that was launched by CBS, as Scooby. Timothy then went on to play a short role in the television show “High Incident”. There were some other shows too where he played such roles.

Timothy Olyphant also did a guest appearance in “Sex and the City”. He made his film debut with a small part in the movie “The First Wives Club”. However, it was with the movie “Scream 2” where he received mainstream attention. He recently played the role of the protagonist in the film “Hitman”. Timothy has played as Raylan Givens in the FX series “Justified”.

Born in Honolulu, Hawaii, Olyphant now lives in Westwood, Los Angeles. He married in July 1991 and has three children. He is the son of Katherine and John Vernon Bevan Olyphant.

His father was a college teacher. He began competitive swimming at the age of six. He also participated in the National swimming championship in 1986 – in the 200 individual medleys category. Tim studied fine arts at the University of Southern California in Los Angeles and took acting classes with William Esper in his studio.
