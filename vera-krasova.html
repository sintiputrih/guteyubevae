Biography

Vera Krasova was born in Moscow, Russia on December 11, 1987. Russian model and TV presenter who became known after competing in Miss Russia 2007. She was the first Russian to place in the top 5 of Miss Universe 2008 since Oxana Fedorova in 2002. Her first beauty pageant was Miss Moscow 2007 where she won First Vice Miss Moscow 2007.
