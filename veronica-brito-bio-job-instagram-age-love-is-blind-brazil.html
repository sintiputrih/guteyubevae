Verônica Brito On Love Is Blind Brazil Season 2

Verônica Brito made her television acting debut in the second season of Love Is Blind: Brazil, which debuted in late 2022. The program debuted on December 28.

Like in previous seasons, it was forbidden for the contestants to meet in person; instead, they spoke via audio-connected pods. They connected emotionally for the first time and decided to get engaged without knowing how they looked. The Love Is Blind Brazilian season is hosted by Camila Queiro and Klebber Toledo. It will now be presented how ten Brazilians fell in love while watching the show honeymooned in the Amazon Rainforest.

The other contestants joining the show are Alisson Hentges, Flávia Queiroz, Guilherme Martins, Maíra Bullos, Robert Richard, Thamara Térez, Tiago Augusto, Vanessa Carvalho, William Domiêncio, and Paulo Lopes.

Verônica Brito Job

Verônica Brito recognized herself as a model and a businesswoman. On her professional page, Veronica stated that she started her modeling career at the age of 15 in Sao Paulo. She added, “In the conquest process, I overcame several challenges imposed by the market, covering several segments of fashion and beauty. Today I stand out as a commercial model, doing editorials, advertising, and campaigns for big brands.”

Additionally, starting in 2019, she has been offering personalized training for models. The program aims at people who want to learn how to position themselves in front of cameras for photos and videos. She also offered classes at Vebrito Class which is no longer available.

In the course of working on several modeling jobs, Veronica has also worked on several commercials.

Speaking of her academic qualifications, Veronica studied Technical in Dramatic Art at Senac São Paulo.
