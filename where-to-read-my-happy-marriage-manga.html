Where to Read My Happy Marriage Manga? A Guide for Fans

Publish date: 2024-08-06

My Happy Marriage is a manga that comes highly recommended for enthusiasts of historical and slow-burn romance. The story’s heartfelt narration and intricately developed characters make it a captivating read, further enriched by the visually stunning artwork that brings the tale to life.

Within My Happy Marriage, the plot delves into important themes like abuse, trauma, and redemption. Our brave protagonist, Miyo Saimori, triumphs over her past, embarking on a journey that leads her to find happiness alongside the male lead, Kiyoka Kudou. This emotionally engaging narrative promises to leave a lasting impact on readers, exploring themes of love, redemption, and self-discovery.

A Guide to Reading My Happy Marriage Manga and Additional Details

Readers can access the manga adaptation, beautifully illustrated by Rito Kohsaka, through Square Enix’s Gangan Online service. Serialized online since December 20, 2018, it has now seen the release of four volumes. English-speaking fans can also rejoice as Square Enix has acquired the license for English publication.

For those looking to read My Happy Marriage online, several options are available:

  • Square Enix’s Gangan Online website or app: The official platform for the serialized manga. Please note that the website is in Japanese, and the app may have limited availability in certain regions.
  • Manga UP!: A legitimate manga-reading app offering a free trial. Readers can enjoy the initial chapters of My Happy Marriage for free, followed by a subscription to access the full story.
  • Amazon Kindle: An excellent alternative for readers who prefer owning physical copies. The manga volumes can be purchased on Amazon Kindle.

    Plot Overview of My Happy Marriage

    Meet Miyo Saimori, a young woman bereft of any supernatural abilities in a family where everyone possesses such gifts. Disconnected from acceptance and support, she bears the burden of manual labor, feeling like an outcast. As she comes of age and becomes eligible for marriage, fate leads her to a precarious chance—an audition as a potential bride for Kiyoka Kudou.

    Kiyoka, known for both kindness and ruthlessness, belongs to one of the most influential families in the land. Fueled by desperation and driven by fear, Miyo sees this as her only opportunity to liberate herself from her abusive family ties.

    In time, love blossoms between Miyo and Kiyoka. However, their path is fraught with obstacles. Kiyoka’s family staunchly opposes their union, making relentless attempts to keep them apart. Moreover, Miyo must confront the lingering scars of her past trauma. Nevertheless, their unwavering commitment to each other empowers them to overcome every hurdle in their way.

    Other Adaptations of My Happy Marriage

    My Happy Marriage has been adapted into an anime series and a live-action series:

    The anime adaptation premiered on Netflix on July 5, 2023. Animated by Kinema Citrus and directed by Takehiro Kubota, the 12-episode series has garnered praise for its stunning animation.

    The live-action series made its debut on TV Tokyo on March 17, 2023. Directed by Ayuko Tsukahara, it features Nana Komatsu as Miyo Saimori and Yuta Koseki as Kiyoka Kuda. Consisting of 10 episodes, the series is available for streaming exclusively on Netflix Japan.

    Both the anime and live-action adaptations have been lauded by manga fans for their exceptional portrayals and faithful adherence to the source material.
