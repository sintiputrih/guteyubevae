Who are Mike Lindell's children?

Publish date: 2024-07-28

MYPILLOW CEO Mike Lindell has been married twice in his life.

The entrepreneur’s second marriage to Dallas Yocum reportedly only lasted one month and he was rumored to have been dating actress Jane Krakowski, though both denied ever having a relationship.

Who are Mike Lindell's children?

Mike Lindell, 59, was previously married to Karen Dickey with whom he shares four children: Heather Lueth, Lizzy Meyers, Darren, and Charlie Lindell.

All four appeared in TV documentary The Mike Lindell Story: An American Dream in which he said his children were the first to be impacted by his MyPillow dream.

Lizzy revealed she was the first to see her father's idea for MyPillow and drawings of the logo and told her dad it was "really random" that he was making pillows now.

Heather told the documentary how her dad arrived at her college where she was studying for a fine arts degree and asked for her help with the logo.

She now works as a Graphic Designer for MyPillow based in Chaska, Minnesota.

Son Darren told how at the start he was put to work by his dad into making prototype pillows, while Charlie admitted that he got involved later down the line hiring his friends to help get the product made.

Son Darren said his father's attitude was to "keep going" even at his lowest point and that he knew he would make a success of it.

And he has gone on to find his own success at his father's company as in November 2019, MyPillow posted on Facebook: "Congratulations to Darren Lindell! He is now the new Chief Operating Officer at MyPillow!"

Charlie told the documentary that a lot of people thought his dad was "crazy" but his dad "persevered".

It is thought he is a salesman with the company.

Mike praised his kids saying: "They've made it possible from day one and it's been a miracle. I can't thank them enough."

Lindell was married to Dickey for over two decades prior to filing for divorce.

When did Dallas Yocum marry Mike Lindell?

Lindell and Yocum married in June2013 but divorced a month later.

The couple reportedly had a prenuptial agreement and Yocum was rumored to have left Lindell.

It is unclear what ultimately led to their split. 

She reportedly keeps her life low-key and away from social media platforms but rose to prominence through Lindell.

Yocum and Lindell did not have any children together while they were married.

He said: “I lost 20 retailers, and it’s cost me $65million this year that I won’t get back, OK?.
