Young Illegal Miners Unearth One of The Largest Gold Nuggets In Ghanas History

Publish date: 2024-09-19

A group of illegal miners have unearthed one of the largest gold nuggets in the history of Ghana.

A group of young illegal miners have have discovered one of the biggest gold nuggets ever found in Ghana.

Whiles digging the earth in one of their Galamsey explorations, they came across a very huge gold nugget.

The gold nugget is believed to be one of the biggest to ever been discovered in the history of the country.

The guys videoed it as they tried to pull it to the earth surface.
