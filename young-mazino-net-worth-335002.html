About

Korean-American actor who rose to fame for his portrayal of Paul Cho in the critically-acclaimed, comedy-drama series Beef. His other work includes minor roles in well-known series like Blue Bloods, Prodigal Son, and New Amsterdam.

He attended the Stella Adler Studio of Acting in 2017 and trained in theatre.

He was interviewed on an April 2023 episode of The Tonight Show. He signed with the M88 representation firm in the year 2023. He appeared in a music video for SZA.

He grew up in the suburbs of Maryland. His real name is Christopher Young Kim.

He worked with Steven Yeun and Ali Wong on the cast of Beef.
