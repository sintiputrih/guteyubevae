Zach Ertz and Julie Ertz's Journey from Sports Stars to Relationship Goals

Publish date: 2024-05-22

Power couples dominate the word of sports and captivate the minds of fans and onlookers like.

Zach Ertz, Super Bowl Champion with the Philadelphia Eagles, and Julie Ertz, World Cup Champion with the United States Women's National Soccer Team, have the perfect blend of athletic dominance and adoration for one another.

It indeed highlights the setting of love, commitment, and making a difference on and off the field as two sports legends journey from individual stars to real relationship goals.

The Start of an Amazing Adventure

Zach and Julie first met at a baseball game in 2012 the start of a love story.

But despite the hurdles of growing their relationship as their athletic careers skyrocketed, the two began to fall for each other.

That first chance meeting in 2012 began a series of milestones and, dozens of shared successes both personal and professional.

Personal Bio and Sports Accomplishments

Zach Ertz: Football Pioneer

One facet of Zach Ertz's football life that has always been prominent is his work ethic.

He broke out at Stanford University and was taken in the second round of the NFL draft by the Philadelphia Eagles and began a special professional path.

His key role in helping the Philadelphia Eagles win the Super Bowl is a testament to his drop dead great body control and the toughness of a player committed unconditionally to his sport. 

A Soccer Star on the Field: Julie Ertz

Julie, however, has done some pretty special things on the soccer field.

After making her debut for the USWNT in 2013 and being named to the winning 2015 World Cup squad, Julie has more than made her mark on the game.

Her commitment, skill and leadership have not only made her one of the top tier of women's footballers but a role model for aspiring athletes the world over.

Changing the perspectives of others by leading both on the field and off the field

Pulling Love Back Onto the Court

Their emotional support for each other (like Julie breaking down in tears when she heard that Zach's team had won while she was playing for the US National team) is what really makes their journey as a couple so special.

Cultivating A Marriage In the Middle of Career Pressures

Zach and Julie both balance the constraints of professional sports and a marriage.

Julie spoke very highly of her marriage, calling it a "gift" and noting that having that clarity certainly helps in understanding what it would take for the other to reach the top of their sport.

Their resilience to work things out in different ways has been inspiring, especially in the midst of a season impacted by the complexities of COVID-19 and their matching resolve to be individually better all while sticking together.

More than Sports Philanthropy and Inspiration

Zach and Julie are widely recognized off the field as much as they are known for their talents on it.

They established the Ertz Family Foundation in 2018 with a mission to bridge sports and education for youth, and restore a sense of hope for families in need.

Their history of leaving things better than they found it is a wisdom shared with those they have been connected to these last two decades as they are incredibly rooted in their community.

On the whole

The couple's journey from sports superstars to relationship goals is a story of true love, commitment and unwavering support.

Their accomplishments as individuals, joint victories and mutual respect for each other have provided generations of people both in and out of the world of sport with a model from which to take guidance.

The Ertz's look to raise the bar in what we know a married couple to be, both on and off the field, and show the world how love, respect, and mutual goals between a pair can pay dividends and reach new heights.
